•	IOT Based Fire Department Alerting System: IOT based automatic fire department alerting system that instantly and automatically alerts the fire department and informs about the situation so that immediate action can be taken. The system uses Fire sensor along with PIR sensor to efficiently detect fires and alert fire department over IOT. 
•	Arduino Based Fire Fighting Robot: Fire Fighting Robot using Arduino, which will automatically sense the fire and start the water pump. This is very simple robot that could move towards the fire and pump out water around it to put down the fire
